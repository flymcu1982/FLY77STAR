# Distances Character Reference Notes

## Female Character

Reference file:

- `Reference/Characters/FEMALE_character_reference.png`

Visual identity:

- Young Japanese woman.
- Long dark hair with soft bangs.
- Cream knit cardigan over a white inner top.
- Gray pleated skirt.
- White socks and white sneakers.
- Gentle, quiet expression.

Emotional use:

- She should not be presented as an idol pose or fashion model.
- Her feeling is held inward: hesitation, memory, warmth, and the fear of saying too much.
- Use small gestures: lowering her eyes, holding her hands behind her back, pausing before walking, turning slightly away.

## Male Character

Reference file:

- `Reference/Characters/MALE_character_reference.png`

Visual identity:

- Young Japanese man.
- Dark textured hair.
- Black/brown plaid overshirt over a white T-shirt.
- Loose blue denim.
- Black shoes.
- Quiet, grounded presence.

Emotional use:

- He should not look like he is posing for introduction.
- His feeling is restrained: loneliness, unfinished words, regret, and a small decision to move closer.
- Use small gestures: hand in pocket, looking away, shallow breath, stopping before speaking.

## Shared Direction

- The MV is not about character reveal.
- The MV is about emotional distance.
- Keep the camera observational, not performative.
- Avoid flashy effects, exaggerated acting, and direct exposition.
- Always choose reality.
