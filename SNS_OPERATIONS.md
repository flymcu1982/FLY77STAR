## Overview

This document defines the SNS account strategy, handle reservation procedure, and implementation checklist for FLY77STAR social media presence across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

**Core Principle**: Unified handle across all platforms where possible: `@FLY77STAR_STUDIO`

---

## SNS Handle Strategy

### Rationale

FLY77STAR. is a **next-generation creative studio** encompassing:
- Music & film production
- Storytelling & narrative
- AI-generated content
- Web presence & community
- Internal wiki (Obsidian)
- AI team collaboration

The **STUDIO** designation (`@FLY77STAR_STUDIO`) accurately reflects this comprehensive identity as a production studio, not merely an artist account.

### Handle Hierarchy

**Priority 1 (Recommended):** `@FLY77STAR_STUDIO`
- Reflects studio/production identity
- Professional tier across platforms
- Scalable for team expansion

**Priority 2 (Fallback):** `@FLY77STAR_OFFICIAL`
- Emphasizes official status
- Acceptable if Priority 1 unavailable

**Priority 3 (Last Resort):** `@FLY77STAR`
- Base brand name only
- Use only if both above unavailable

**New Account Creation**

- Display Name: `FLY77STAR.` (matching YouTube)
- Bio: "🎬✨ Music × Storytelling × AI. Imagining the future. Creating it now." (platform-native brevity)

**Content Strategy:**
