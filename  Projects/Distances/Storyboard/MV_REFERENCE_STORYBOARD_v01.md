# Distances MV Reference Storyboard v01

## Core Intention

近くにいるのに届かない距離、離れているのに消えない感情を描く。

This storyboard uses the supplied male and female character references as visual anchors. The goal is not to introduce them as models. The goal is to let the viewer feel a quiet distance between two people.

## Global Visual Rules

- Format: vertical 9:16.
- Look: Kodak Vision3, Blue Hour, warm skin, natural contrast.
- Lens feel: 85mm, shallow depth of field, quiet static camera.
- Movement: minimal, human, unforced.
- Transitions: mostly cuts. Only use soft fades at the opening or ending if needed.
- Acting: do not perform sadness. Hold it.
- Negative: no flashy VFX, no heavy glow, no music-video posing, no rapid montage.
