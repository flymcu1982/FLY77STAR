- [ ] All account profiles are set (name, bio, links, icons)

- [ ] Email recovery settings confirmed for all accounts
- [ ] 2FA enabled on Gmail / recommended on social accounts
- [ ] Social platform links are functional (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, official site)
- [ ] Hashtag strategy reviewed and finalized
- [ ] All posts reviewed for grammar, tone, brand consistency
